About the Conference

Diplomacy Starts Here

TAMS MUN 2026 is hosted by the Texas Academy of Mathematics and Science at the University of North Texas in Denton, Texas. This conference brings together high school students from across the state for two days of rigorous debate, coalition-building, and international policy-making.

Whether you're a first-time delegate or a seasoned MUN veteran, TAMS MUN 2026 offers a challenging and rewarding environment to develop your public speaking, research, and critical thinking skills.

Our experienced secretariat and faculty advisors are committed to running an inclusive, educational, and professionally organized conference that reflects the standards of real United Nations proceedings.

University Setting

Experience conference sessions held in the university facilities of UNT's Denton campus.

All Experience Levels

Committees are structured for beginner, intermediate, and advanced delegates. Every school and every experience level is welcome.

Awards & Recognition

Outstanding delegates, best position papers, and best delegations will be recognized at the closing ceremony on the final day.

Hosted by TAMS Students

Organized entirely by current TAMS students — a student-run conference bringing peer-to-peer learning in international affairs.
