Diplomacy Starts Here

TAMS MUN 2026 is hosted by the Texas Academy of Mathematics and Science at the University of North Texas in Denton, Texas. This conference brings together high school students from across the state for two days of rigorous debate and policy making.

TAMS MUN 2026 offers a challenging and rewarding environment to develop your public speaking, research, and critical thinking skills.

Experience conference sessions held in the university facilities of UNT's Denton campus.

Committees are structured for beginner, intermediate, and advanced delegates. Every school and every experience level is welcome.

Outstanding delegates, best position papers, and best delegations will be recognized at the closing ceremony on the final day.
